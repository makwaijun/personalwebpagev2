Skills and Experience

An alternate take on a Resume

Underlying theory:

There are four big pillars of domains (top 25% of world). This could be things you have the most experience in/ way to best add value to the world. This creates a unique mix of capabilities to add value to the world. Roughly - it fulfils (i) Differentiated enough to be considered good enough professionally (Ii) The world will pay me for it

Beneath these, there are X supporting blocks (above average, top 50%). These could be hobbies/ passion projects. ROughly- (i) I am above average with a good amount of certainty, (ii) Can hold intelligent and engaging conversations on this at my discretion, (iii) not easily paid for it, whether due to the nature of the work, or lack of formal capability.

Finally, there are certain areas that i Need to improve myself in, to be above average. Roughly (i) weaknesses i am aware of, (ii) useful, complementary and desirable things
