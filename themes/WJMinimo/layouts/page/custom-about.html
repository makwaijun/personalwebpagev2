About Me

The Person

I'm a confused Singaporean striving to learn widely and apply deeply. Inclined to the former, struggling with the latter. Theory is great but skin-in-the-game is better. I stray away from viewing the world in black or white . Living in the gray sits right with me. To stay sane, being principled is my guiding light in a fog of ambiguity.

I consider myself a generalist, with the ambition to be above average in a unique mix of domains.

You can find me feeding on a diet of current affairs, general knowledge, memes and reddit.

The Site

This personal (web) project aims to be a node of interconnecting thoughts and interests. Part diary, part time capsule, part contemplation.
