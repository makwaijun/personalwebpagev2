To feel overwhelmed  
Til the downtime   

Then the silence  

Deafeningly heard   
by the stasis of   
being down   

During  
the Down, down time.   

*Context: thematic on finding down time in our busy lives, and then feeling bad during such times when one is disengaged, and so the cycle continues. This feeling featured a little bit more in the 2022/23*
