To Fall Backward  
Into the pitch of darkness  
The loss of perception  
The realms of despair  
The despised desparation

To Feel Forward  
To do right righteously  
To bask in and face the sun  
To lean into the tasks
that no one asked for,  
 no one wanted to do.   

To See beyond  
The Horizon   
Past the clouds of the Past  
Lightly, do I tread on the Path  
of ever-imperiled passion  

Never to mourn the loss  
nor betray, one's precious integrity
